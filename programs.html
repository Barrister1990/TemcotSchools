Programs/ Activities

  • Computer Coding

    Our Computer Coding program equips students with essential programming skills, fostering creativity and problem-solving to prepare them for a future in technology

  • Reading and Debate Clubs

    At Temcot Schools, our Reading and Debate Clubs encourage critical thinking and effective communication. Students engage in lively discussions and explore diverse literature, enhancing their analytical skills and confidence

  • Sports and Physical Education

    Temcot Schools’ Sports and Physical Education program fosters teamwork and fitness through diverse activities. We promote healthy lifestyles and believe that sports build character and resilience in students

  • Abacus

    The Abacus program at Temcot Schools boosts math skills through engaging activities. Students enhance mental calculation and critical thinking, fostering a love for math and building confidence for academic success

  • Foreign Exchange Programme

    Our Foreign Exchange Programme offers students a unique opportunity to experience diverse cultures, broaden their horizons, and enhance global understanding.

  • Robotics

    Our Robotics program introduces students to the world of engineering and automation, encouraging innovation and hands-on learning in cutting-edge technology.

At Temcot Schools, we nurture future leaders with a strong foundation in academics, creativity, and personal growth. Enroll your child today and watch them excel in a supportive and dynamic learning environment. Temcot Schools – where bright futures begin
