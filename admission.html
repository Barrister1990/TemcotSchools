Details on the Admission Process

Kindly download The Application form or visit out School Reception to fill out the Admission forms

Parents will be required to pay an Admission Fee to begin the Admission Process.

All new admissions will be required to take part in an Entrance Examination to determine the their performance in various areas.

The following documents are required for the Admission Process:

  • A current Report Card or
  • Cumulative Record book from previous school.
  • Identification Card
  • Passport Picture
  • A medical Report if necessary
